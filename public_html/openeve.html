Have you had an interest in photography for a long time or only just begun? It doesn't matter which, you will find a welcoming and enthusiastic group at Bromsgrove Photographic Society.

Do you have questions you need answered or help with your photography? Come along on 31st August and meet us.
We have a session led by Peter Young, our president, giving advice and helpful suggestions about pictures brought in on the evening. There is also a short session looking at the website and outlining other online facilities for members to access and use.
