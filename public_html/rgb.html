sRGB v Adobe RGB and Photoshop

After Peter's comments about not getting jpeg files quite right for PDI, I enquired where the mistakes were. In my case it was with the colour space of the image. DiCentra, the siftware we use for displaying images in the Projected competitions, has it's colour space set to sRGB.

This set me off checking what I had my camera and Photoshop set to. I also decided to investigate what Nikon had to say about the colour space, I'm a Nikon user.

Put simply the for printing Adobe RGB would appear to be best, for Web or PDI sRGB would appear to be best, but, not all the web sites I looked at say exactly the same. Here are a couple of links I found on the web. There are loads, have a browse yourself.
